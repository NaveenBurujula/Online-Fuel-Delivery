Ad Choices

At Nans Online Fuel Services, we value your privacy and are committed to providing you with transparency and control over your online advertising preferences. Our Ad Choices page allows you to learn more about online advertising, how it works, and how you can manage your ad preferences.

What is Online Advertising?

Online advertising is a form of marketing that uses the internet to deliver promotional messages to users. It includes various types of ads such as display ads, search ads, social media ads, and video ads.

Managing Your Ad Preferences

You can manage your ad preferences and opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting the AdChoices website or adjusting the settings in your browser or device. Additionally, you can opt-out of specific ad networks or ad targeting by visiting the websites of individual advertising companies.
